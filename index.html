If you like Egg Nog and coffee, try this recipe.

Ingredients

⅓ cup 2% milk

⅔ cup eggnog

1 (1.5 fluid ounce) jigger brewed espresso

1 pinch ground nutmeg

This recipe is from allrecipes.com


Steps

  1. Pour milk and eggnog into a steaming pitcher and heat to between 145 degrees F to 165 degrees F (65 to 70 degrees C) using the steaming wand.

  2. Brew the shot of espresso, then add to mug. Pour the steamed milk and eggnog into the mug, using a spoon to hold back the foam. Spoon foam over the top. Sprinkle nutmeg on top of the foam.
